Dialogflow Chatbot Demo

Helps extract flood related information from a SWMM model

This chatbot extracts information from a SWMM model for the purposes of identifying surcharged and flooded pipes in the Do Lo-Yen Nghia ward in Hanoi Vietnam. The chatbot is a prototype to enable easier and faster access to some SWMM model results. The following can be accessed:

  • the surcharged pipes in the network
  • the flooded pipes in the network
  • the location of the flooded pipes
  • the time series of depth of water in a pipe
  • the time series of velocity of flow in a pipe
  • the area of the network
  • the link and node IDs


Study Area

Do Lo-Yen Nghia, Ha Dong District, Hanoi, Vietnam

Do Lo lies to the south of the Yen Nghia ward in the Ha Dong District of Hanoi, the capital city of Vietnam. The average temperature is 23℃, with the lowest being 5℃ and the highest 38℃. Do Lo, Yen Nghia is about 61.5 ha in area and is located next to the Yen Nghia dyke. It is this dyke that protects the area from the Day River which lies to the west of the ward. The Co Ban channel, located to the South and the East of Do Lo, is the main output of the drainage system. The area is experiencing rapid population growth which in turn puts a strain on the urban infrastructure.
